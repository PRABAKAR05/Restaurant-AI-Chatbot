LEGEND:
  V    = Vegetarian
  GF   = Gluten-Free
  Vegan = No dairy, no animal products
  Jain = No onion, no garlic (available on request)

🌿 VEGAN OPTIONS:
   Items 6, 19, 20, 23, 37, 38 and all beverages 
   marked Vegan can be prepared without any 
   dairy or animal products.

🌾 GLUTEN-FREE OPTIONS:
   Most of our curries, rice dishes, and grilled 
   items are naturally gluten-free. Items marked GF 
   are confirmed gluten-free. Please inform your 
   server about allergies.

🕉️ JAIN OPTIONS:
   Items 23 (Aloo Gobi) and select dishes can be 
   prepared without onion and garlic on request. 
   Please inform your server when ordering.

⚠️ ALLERGEN INFO:
   Our kitchen uses nuts, dairy, gluten, and 
   shellfish. Please inform your server about 
   any food allergies or dietary restrictions.
